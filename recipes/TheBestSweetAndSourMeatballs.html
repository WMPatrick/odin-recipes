The Best Sweet and Sour Meatballs

Description

Delicious sweet and sour meatballs made with ingredients you always have around the house! A definite winner! Wonderful served with rice!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 egg
  • Ginger
  • 1/4 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Mix beef, onion, egg, and bread crumbs together in a medium bowl until well combined.
  2. Shape mixture into balls, about 1 1/2-inches in size.
  3. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes; set aside.
  4. Stir water, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, and flour together in a large saucepan until well combined.
  5. Add meatballs and bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring often, for 30 minutes.
  6. Add the rice into the pot. Pour in the stock and meat chunks. Add more water so there is enough to cook the rice (read the rice pack instructions). Taste for salt and add more if needed.
  7. Cover the allow the rice to cook until all the moisture is absorbed.
